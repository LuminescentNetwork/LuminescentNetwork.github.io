Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

1. Introduction

Welcome to Luminescent Network. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and servers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Minecraft Username
  • Profile information (nickname, avatar)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To improve our website and services
  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. How We Protect Your Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Business partners with your consent
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Other users (information you choose to make public)

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Some features may not work properly if you disable cookies.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of promotional communications
  • Request a copy of your data

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our online services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information (if collected outside of minecraft). Our Minecraft servers will generally follow Minecraft's age rating, 10+.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside your country. Your use of our services constitutes your consent to such transfers.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of our services constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
